Two-player Poker Card Game

Player's cards can be flipped by clicking any time during the game.

Each game consists of 4 rounds, where the first round has no cards flipped. In each round, players take turns making a bet, then common cards are revealed.

In each player's turn, the player can click to view their cards. Players bet tokens by selecting tokens and pressing the 'Make Bet!' button.

After each player has taken a turn, press the 'Next Step' button to flip common cards and proceed to the next round.

Simplified rules:

For each turn, there are maximum two cycles. During the first cycle through all the players, raises are allowed. In the second cycle (if needed), only matches are allowed.

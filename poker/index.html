Multi-player Poker Card Game

Refresh page until pop-up window asking for username appears!

Instructions:

  • Player's cards can be flipped by clicking any time during the game.
  • Each game consists of 4 rounds, where round 0 has no cards flipped, round 1 flips first three cards, round 2 flips fourth card, and round 3 flips last card. In each round, players take turns making a bet. The common cards are automatically revealed once a round is finished.
  • In each player's turn, the players can bet tokens by selecting tokens and pressing the 'Make Bet!' button. The player can also choose to fold and quit this game.
  • Throughout the game, there will be a message area on the webpage displaying the current state of the game and reminder for what to do. The text will be shown in bright blue for contrast.
  • Once all rounds are done, a pop-up window will display the winner. This message will also be shown in the message area of the webpage. Then, winners can collect tokens and non-winners can clear the tokens by pressing the corresdoing buttons.
  • A new game will be initiated once all players have clicked the 'Reset Game' button.

Simplified rules:

  • For each turn, there are maximum two cycles. During the first cycle through all the players, raises are allowed. In the second cycle (if needed), only matches are allowed.
  • Play order is determined by the order of users entering their username. Play order will be rotated in new games.
